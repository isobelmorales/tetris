Tetris

Instructions

Move and rotate the falling blocks to fit them in the game board.

To clear a line, fill every square within a single row.

Earn points by clearing lines.

If the blocks reach the top of the board, the game will end.

Controls

  • Rotate right
  • Rotate left
  • Hard Drop
  • Soft Drop
  • Move left
  • Move right
